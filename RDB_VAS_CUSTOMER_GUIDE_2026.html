Paint Protection Film (PPF)
🎨
Physical Paint Protection · Best Applied Before First Drive

An invisible shield. Showroom paint, preserved for years.

Paint Protection Film is a transparent, thermoplastic urethane film applied over your car's paint. Stone chips, road debris, minor scratches, UV fading, acid rain, and chemical stains — all absorbed before they reach your paint. Premium grades are self-healing: minor surface scratches disappear with warmth. Best applied before your car's first drive, so the paint it protects is truly factory-fresh.

Ceramic Coating
💎
9H Ceramic · Hydrophobic · 2–5 Year Durability

A glass-like shield. Deeper shine. Rain rolls right off.

Ceramic coating creates a permanent, chemically bonded, hydrophobic layer over your car's paint. Rain, mud, and dust don't stick — they bead and roll off. The coating gives your paint a deep, wet-look gloss that turns heads. Washing takes half the time, and your paint is protected from chemicals, UV, and light scratches. Available in 2-year to 5-year grades.
